Question No: 6 · Simulation — WAN Health & Network Monitoring
After a recent power outage, users are reporting performance issues accessing application servers. Wireless users are also reporting intermittent Internet issues. Review the network health dashboard and device monitoring data, then answer the associated questions using the drop-down menus.

Instructions

  • Click on the Network Health and Device Monitoring tabs to view performance widgets.
  • Analyze WAN metrics: uplink speed, packet loss, latency, and jitter.
  • For each question, select the best answer from the dropdown. VoIP requires low latency, low jitter, and minimal packet loss — not necessarily the highest bandwidth.
